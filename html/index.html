About

Due to their limited resources, much of independent radio is not syndicated or available after broadcast. We want to enjoy the benefits of time-shifted programming on these stations.

How it works

The web stream of the station is recorded and then saved and delivered for personal use. Every station is a different server which acts in a federation.

Who runs this

People like you. Voluntarily.

Join the Federation

Each server gets a hostname corresponding to the callsign of the station. For instance, kxlu.indycast.net and kdvs.indycast.net correspond to different servers which are responsible for those stations. Some machines of course, may support multiple stations.

If you'd like to add or support a station, you can join the federation by reading up on it here.
