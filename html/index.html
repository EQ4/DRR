About

Due to their limited resources, much of independent radio is not syndicated or available after broadcast. We want to enjoy the benefits of time-shifted programming on these stations.

How it works

The web stream of the station is recorded and then saved for personal use. Because of networking and storage overhead, only a limited number of stations are supported.
